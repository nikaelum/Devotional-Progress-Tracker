📖 SOAP Method Guide

▲
S

Scripture

Choose a passage to study. Write it out word for word. Reading it slowly and intentionally helps you absorb the text. Consider reading it in multiple translations.

O

Observation

What stands out to you? Look at the context—who wrote it, to whom, and why. Note repeated words, commands, promises, warnings, or themes. What is God communicating?

A

Application

How does this passage apply to your life today? Is there a command to obey, a promise to claim, a sin to avoid, or an example to follow? Be specific and personal.

P

Prayer

Respond to God in prayer based on what you read. Ask for help applying His word. Thank Him for what He revealed. Pour out your heart honestly before Him.
